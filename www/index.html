Recepie

1. Mix water and starter.

2. Mix in flour and salt.

3. Let rest for 30 - 60 min.

4. Do one strech and fold. For the next 2h perform strech and fold each 30 min.

5. Let rest over night for bettter taste, or proceed to next step immedately.

6. Put pot in oven and set to 250C.

6. Pour dough on kitchen bench and form to a ball by rotating the mass with a dough cutter. Let rest for 30 min.

7. From dough to a ball and cover with flour.

8. Put dough in pot and put lid on for 30-40minutes, depending on bread size.

9. Let bread cool under blanket for 60min
